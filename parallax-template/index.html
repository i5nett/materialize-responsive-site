Learn from us, Dance like monkeys

Characters in the anime series Overman King Gainer do the monkey in the opening animation and in the show itself.Bend forward from waist to the left, raising right arm. As your body bobs, your head also bobs forward on each count. The whole effect is jerky. Straighten up to original position. Bend forward from waist toward your partner, facing centre, switching arms as you do so. Straighten to original position. Hands and head should give impression of monkey holding two bananas. Bend forward from waist to the right. Straighten to original position. Bob back to centre, bending at waist and again switching hands. Repeat entire pattern. Counts are double time, hitting every accent in the music.
